ABOUT

About Me

I am Anil Kumar Shaw, currently serving as a Scientist-D with a 13+ years of experience in the field of IoT. My academic background includes an M.Tech in Information Technology and a B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering. With expertise in Digital Signal Processing, I specialize in the integration of Internet of Things (IoT) and Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIoT) applications, particularly at the edge.

My research interests extend to EdgeAI, Embedded Systems, and their amalgamation with IoT technologies. This involves exploring the potential of signal processing techniques within these domains. At NIELIT Guwahati and its Extension Centres, I undertake diverse roles encompassing academic teaching, electronics, and hardware. Additionally, I serve as the Examination Head and Vigilance Officer, ensuring the integrity and quality of our examination processes.

Dedicated to advancing the fields of Digital Signal Processing, IoT, EdgeAI, and Embedded Systems, I eagerly look forward to continued contributions and collaborations in these areas.
